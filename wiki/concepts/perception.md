# Perception

Perception covers scene understanding, representation extraction, sensor fusion, and world-state construction for downstream autonomy.

## Common subproblems

- detection,
- tracking,
- occupancy and scene representation,
- lane and map element extraction,
- multi-view geometry,
- lidar-camera fusion,
- bird's-eye-view construction.

## Evaluation caution

Perception progress is often real but narrow. The wiki should track when benchmark gains do not meaningfully improve downstream prediction or planning.
