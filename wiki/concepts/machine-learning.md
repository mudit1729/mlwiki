# Machine Learning

This page is the top-level anchor for the ML side of the vault.

## Scope

Relevant slices of ML for this wiki:

- deep learning architectures,
- self-supervised and multimodal pretraining,
- sequence modeling,
- imitation and reinforcement learning,
- uncertainty estimation,
- representation learning for control and decision making.

## Why it matters here

Autonomous driving and robotics papers often present themselves as systems work, but their real contribution usually lands in one of a few ML buckets: representation learning, action-conditioned prediction, generative modeling, multimodal grounding, or optimization under safety constraints.
