# Planning

Planning turns scene understanding and future hypotheses into actions.

## Levels

- route planning,
- behavior planning,
- trajectory planning,
- control.

## What the wiki should track

- whether planning is explicit or latent,
- whether it is optimization-based, learned, or hybrid,
- whether evaluation is closed-loop,
- how safety constraints are represented,
- whether the planner reasons over uncertainty or collapses it.
