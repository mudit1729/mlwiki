# Robotics

Robotics is important here for transfer, not because driving reduces to robotics.

## Useful transfers

- action-conditioned multimodal modeling,
- instruction grounding,
- large-scale behavior cloning,
- foundation-model interfaces for perception-to-action,
- world-model and latent-control abstractions.

## Where transfer breaks

- driving has far less tolerance for exploratory behavior,
- long-horizon road interaction differs from manipulation episodes,
- evaluation in robotics often under-specifies adversarial multi-agent traffic behavior,
- sensor, map, and infrastructure assumptions differ materially.
