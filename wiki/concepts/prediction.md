# Prediction

Prediction covers forecasting the future behavior of other agents and the scene.

## Core themes

- multimodality,
- uncertainty,
- interaction modeling,
- goal-conditioned forecasting,
- scene-level joint prediction,
- map and lane conditioning.

## Practical tension

Prediction metrics can improve while actual planning utility stays flat. This wiki should prefer sources that connect prediction outputs to downstream decision quality.
