This page tracks the foundation-model layer relevant to autonomy research.

## Main buckets

- language models,
- vision-language models,
- multimodal instruction tuning,
- large-scale action-conditioned models,
- world models and generative environment models.

## Questions that matter here

- Which foundation-model ideas improve autonomy directly?
- Which only improve interfaces, tooling, or data synthesis?
- What must be added for safety-critical deployment?
