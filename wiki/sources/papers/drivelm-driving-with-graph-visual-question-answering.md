# DriveLM: Driving with Graph Visual Question Answering

## Citation

## Canonical link

- [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.14150)

## Why it matters

DriveLM is a strong reasoning-oriented bridge from VLMs to driving. Its Graph VQA framing is useful for interpretability and for decomposing perception, prediction, and planning questions.
