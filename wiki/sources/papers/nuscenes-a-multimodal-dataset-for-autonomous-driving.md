# nuScenes: A Multimodal Dataset for Autonomous Driving

## Citation

Caesar et al., CVPR, 2020.

## Canonical link

- [Paper](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content_CVPR_2020/html/Caesar_nuScenes_A_Multimodal_Dataset_for_Autonomous_Driving_CVPR_2020_paper.html)

## Why it matters

nuScenes became a central multimodal benchmark for 3D perception and later planning-oriented autonomy work. It also helped standardize BEV-centric evaluation.
